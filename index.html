About Ten-K Liquor: Your Premier Destination

Ten-K Liquor is your premier destination in Hastings, Minnesota, for a thoughtfully curated selection of fine spirits, exquisite wines, and a diverse range of craft and imported beers. Our unwavering mission is to provide high-quality products at competitive prices, all while delivering exceptional customer service. We are deeply committed to helping you discover exactly what you need, whether it's for a special occasion, a quiet evening, or stocking your home bar.
